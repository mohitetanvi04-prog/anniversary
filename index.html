A letter for you

I don’t really know when it happened. Somewhere between our talks, the silences, and the normal days, you started to matter to me more than I expected. There was no big moment or clear beginning. It just grew quietly. Being with you never felt like work. It felt easy, and it felt right.

With you, I learned that love doesn’t have to be loud or dramatic. It can be calm. It can be patient. You made me feel safe — not in a perfect way, but in a real way. Safe to be myself. Safe to feel things without explaining too much. Safe to grow at my own pace.

This year wasn’t perfect. We learned, adjusted, and tried again. But we stayed. And somewhere along the way, you became home to me — not a place, just a feeling I carry without thinking.

Thank you for staying. For your effort. For your presence. For being real.

Still choosing you.
Even on the ordinary days.

— Tanvi
